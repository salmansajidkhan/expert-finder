# Expert Finder Agent — Feature Launch Summary

**v1.0 | March 2026**

---

## What It Is
An AI-powered agent that finds internal Microsoft experts using live M365 signals.

---

## Features

🔍 **Multi-Signal Expert Search**
Search across people profiles, email threads, meetings, and documents simultaneously. One query, all signals.

⚡ **Fast Search + Deep Search Modes**
Quick results by default. Say "deep search" for parallel multi-signal queries with cross-referenced accuracy.

📊 **Formalized Confidence Scoring**
Every candidate rated High / Medium / Low based on: number of signal types, recency of activity, and role alignment.

🕐 **Recency Weighting**
Candidates labeled Currently Active, Recently Active, Possibly Stale, or Likely Stale — so you never waste time on outdated contacts.

✅ **Org Chart Validation**
Automatic role/team verification flags candidates who may have changed positions.

✉️ **Auto-Generated Outreach Drafts**
Top 2 candidates always include a ready-to-send email with evidence-based personalization.

🔗 **Source Links on Every Result**
Click through to the original email thread, profile, meeting, or document to verify context yourself.

🏷️ **Topic Tags**
Results tagged with relevant domains (e.g., anti-cheat, VPN, partner-escalation, SoundWire) for quick scanning.
